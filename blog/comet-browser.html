🌐 Comet Browser Review & Tips

by Joshua “RedBeard” Smith – RedBeard Technologies LLC

What Makes Comet Stand Out

  • AI-native platform with built-in Perplexity assistant
  • Summarize pages, manage tabs, automate workflows
  • Integrates with Gmail, Calendar, Slack, Notion
  • Access to multiple models (GPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, etc.)

Tips & Tricks

  • AI Sidebar Summaries – instant, cited overviews
  • Autopilot Mode – automate multi-site research
  • @tab hack – compare open tabs instantly
  • Integrations – stay in flow with calendar + Slack
  • Tab Cleanup – ask Comet which tabs to close
  • Context Memory – remembers past searches/actions

Pro Hacks

  • Upload files (PDF, CSV) for instant insights
  • Auto Tab Groups by project
  • Citation mode for research writing
  • Set custom model behavior

Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overloading it with giant prompts
  • Ignoring privacy settings
  • Forgetting voice/file features

Verdict

Comet isn’t just another Chromium reskin—it’s the start of agentic browsing. The sidebar + automation turns the browser into an active assistant.
